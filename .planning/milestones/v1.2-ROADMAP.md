# Milestone v1.2: Trader-Grade Transparency & Validation

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-05-10

**Tag:** `v1.2`

## Overview

Lift the v1.1 hosted dashboard from "tells you what to do" → "shows you exactly why and tracks how it played out". Make every signal *reproducible by hand* and every paper trade *measurable*. Ship a 5-year backtest gate that any future strategy change must pass. Includes a UI/UX overhaul (true two-axis market × function navigation) and a code-quality correctness sweep (Decimal money math, file-size hygiene, naive-datetime fail-closed, schema-migration contiguity).
